How to play

Choose one of the three options and drag it to the answer box, you can fill up to 10 circle in a single answer box.

Press the submit button to submit your answer, make sure that your answer is already right!

If your answer is correct, a green light will spawn on the card. Otherwise, a red light will spawn instead.

Good luck!, reach the highest score you can.
